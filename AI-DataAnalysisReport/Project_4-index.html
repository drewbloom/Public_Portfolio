Comparing Your Program Objectives to New AAMC Competencies

How does it work?

These video instructions will guide you through the process.

Use a file from your Google Drive with "Anyone with the link can view" permissions to provide your program objectives. The file must be a Google Doc or a PDF held on your Google Drive, and the reader will not work unless you hit "Share" - "Anyone with the link can view", then use "Copy link" to submit the document below.


Your file is read by an AI to locate your program objectives. Because there may be errors or inconsistencies due to the AI reader, the objectives located will be included below for your review. Using a text comparison model, those objectives are compared to the new AAMC competencies, and the results are displayed on the webpage below. By hovering your cursor over the AAMC charts, you can see a list of all matching program objectives found for each AAMC competency. Tables are also included beneath each chart to show matching objectives. You can download the webpage as a pdf after the report is generated.


By hitting "Generate Report", you agree to allow Aquifer to compare your program objectives to the new AAMC competencies using a text comparison model and retain the resulting data for future use.
